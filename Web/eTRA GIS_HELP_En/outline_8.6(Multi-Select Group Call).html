Multi-Select Group Call

The dispatch console can combine multiple talkgroups into one multi-select group. The terminals in the group can only receive the call, while they cannot transmit voices.

Click [Dispatch/Call panel] on the menu bar and click “Multi-select group call". The “Multi-select group call” dialog box pops up, shown as below. We can perform related operations through the dialog box.

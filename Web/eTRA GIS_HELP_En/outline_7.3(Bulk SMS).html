Bulk SMS

When an emergency occurs in a region, we can quickly select the police officers near the region, and send mass messages to them to issue the instruction.

The steps of mass texting are as follows:

1) Click "" in the map toolbar and select a region of one or more mobile users. The user list pops up, shown in Figure 1.

Figure 1. Select the users

2) Click <Send SMS>, and the "Send SMS" dialog box pops up, shown in Figure 2. Enter the content and choose whether to request a receipt. Then click <Send>.

Figure 2. Bulk SMS

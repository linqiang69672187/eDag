Map Navigation

The map navigation bar is shown as below.

Map navigation bar

The steps of navigating to each level are as follows:

1) Click a level on the navigation bar to display this level.

2) Click the zoom in button on the navigation bar to zoom the level in.

3) Click the zoom out button on the navigation bar to zoom the level out.

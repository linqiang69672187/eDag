Emergency Half-Duplex Private Call

  • Call Panel: Initiate Emergency Half-Duplex Private Call; Receive Emergency Half-Duplex Private Call

  • Uplink Call Panel: Receive Emergency Half-Duplex Private Call; Initiate Emergency Half-Duplex Private Call

The steps of initiating an emergency half-duplex private call through call panel are as follows:

1) Click [Dispatch/Call panel] on the menu bar and click "Emergency call”. The “Emergency call” dialog box pops up.

2) Enter the terminal ID, such as 180008. The system will get the corresponding information automatically.

3) Select the “Call mode” as “PTT mode”, and click <PTT>, shown in Figure 1.1.

Figure 1.1. The subscriber is ringing

4) After answering the call, the user can hold down the PTT button to speak, shown in Figure 1.2.

Figure 1.2. Mobile user obtains permission

5) After the user releases the PTT button, the dispatcher can press <PTT> to speak.

6) Click <End> to end the call.

The steps of receiving an emergency half-duplex private call through call panel are as follows:

1) If the dispatch console receives an emergency half-duplex call from the user 180008, the “Emergency call” dialog box is shown in Figure 1.3.

Figure 1.3. Call access request

2) Click <PTT>, and then press <PTT> to speak, shown in Figure 1.4.

Figure 1.4. Dispatcher obtains permission

3) After the dispatcher releases <PTT>, the user can hold down the PTT button to speak.

4) Click <End> to end the call.

The steps of receiving an emergency half-duplex private call through uplink call panel are as follows:

1) When the dispatch console receives an emergency half-duplex call from a user, such as user 180008, the private call panel is shown in Figure 2.1.

Figure 2.1. Call access request

2) Click <Answer>, and then press <PTT> to speak, shown in Figure 2.2.

Figure 2.2. Dispatcher obtains permission

3) After the dispatcher releases <PTT>, the user can hold down the PTT button to speak.

4) Click <Hang up> to end the call.

The steps of initiating an emergency half-duplex private call through uplink call panel are as follows:

1) Click <Call> in the private call panel to initiate the call, and press <PTT> to speak.

2) After the dispatcher releases the PTT button, the user can press <PTT> to speak, shown in Figure 2.3.

Figure 2.3. Mobile user obtains permission

3) Click <Hang up> to end the call.

Note:

In the uplink call panel, you can only initiate an emergency half-duplex call to the terminal that already has the emergency half-duplex call records.

Call Service

There are several methods to perform the call service.

1) Right-click a mobile user on the map and select the call service, shown in Figure 1.

Figure 1. Right-click the user and select the call service

2) Click [Dispatch/Call panel] on the menu bar and select the call service on the call panel, shown in Figure 2.

Figure 2. Select the call service on the call panel

3) Make calls through the call toolbar, shown in Figure 3.

Figure 3. Initiate call through call toolbar

4) Select a region on the map, and initiate private call or group call to one of the selected user, shown in Figure 4.

Figure 4. Initiate call through region selection

5) Initiate call through the member tree, shown in Figure 5.

Figure 5. Initiate call through member tree

6) Search the user and initiate call, shown in Figure 6.

Figure 6. Search the user and initiate call

7) Receive calls through the uplink call panel, and initiate calls through the call records, shown in Figure 7.

Figure 7. Make calls through uplink call panel

The call services include:

Private Call

Group Call

Emergency Call

Patch Group Call

Multi-Select Group Call

Base Station Call

Discreet Listening

Break-In

Forced Release

Ambience Listening

Kill/Revive

DGNA

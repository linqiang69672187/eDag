Electronic Fence

We can define an area on the map (the area boundary can be displayed or hidden). Zone the mobile users in this area. When a user leaves the area, the dispatch console will send an alarm.

The steps of adding the electronic fence are as follows:

1) Right-click a mobile user on the map and select [Application service/Electronic fence]. The “Select the electronic fence style” dialog box pops up, shown in Figure 1.

Select the electronic fence style

2) Alternatively, click [Dispatch/Call panel] on the menu bar and click "Electronic fence”. The Electronic Fence List pops up, shown in Figure 2.

Figure 2. Electronic Fence List

3) Click <Add>. The “Select the electronic fence style” dialog box pops up. Select the "Shape", enter the "Name", and select the "Fill Color", "Transparency", "Contour" and "Style". Click <Select> to add one or more users in the electronic fence.

4) Click <OK>, and draw the electronic fence area. Hold down the left mouse button in a starting point, and move to a ending point. Release the mouse, and the system will prompt, “Succeed to add the electronic fence”.

5) The selected users will receive a SMS notification. When a user’s activity area is beyond the electronic fence, the user will receive an alarm, and a prompt will appear beside the user icon.

The steps of modifying the electronic fence are as follows:

1) Click [Dispatch/Call panel] on the menu bar and click "Electronic fence”. The Electronic Fence List pops up.

2) Click <Modify> in the right of the electronic fence. The “Modify” dialog box pops up. Add or delete the users in the electronic fence based on actual needs, shown in Figure 3.

3) Click <OK>. If succeeded, the system will prompt the success; if failed, it will prompt the cause of error.

Figure 3. Modify the electronic fence

The steps of deleting the electronic fence are as follows:

1) Click [Dispatch/Call panel] on the menu bar and click "Electronic fence”. The Electronic Fence List pops up.

2) Click <Delete> in the right of the electronic fence. A confirmation window pops up.

3) Click <OK>. If succeeded, the system will prompt the success; if failed, it will prompt the cause of error.

The steps of viewing the electronic fence are as follows:

1) Click [Dispatch/Call panel] on the menu bar and click "Electronic fence”. The Electronic Fence List pops up.

2) Enter the name of the electronic fence and click <Search>. The list will display the electronic fence information.

3) Click the fence name to display the details, shown in Figure 4.

Figure 4. View the electronic fence information

The steps of locating the electronic fence are as follows:

1) Click [Dispatch/Call panel] on the menu bar and click "Electronic fence”. The Electronic Fence List pops up.

2) Click <Locate> in the right of the electronic fence. The map’s center point will immediately locate to the electronic fence's current position on the map, and mark with special icon, to realize the rapid positioning function.

The steps of displaying/hiding the electronic fence are as follows:

1) Click [Dispatch/Call panel] on the menu bar and click "Electronic fence”. The Electronic Fence List pops up.

2) In the “Display” column, check to display the electronic fence on the map, and uncheck to hide the electronic fence.

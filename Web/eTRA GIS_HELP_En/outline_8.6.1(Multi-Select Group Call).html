The steps of customizing the multi-select group are as follows:

1) Click <Custom> in the “Multi-select” dialog box. The “Add” dialog box pops up, shown in Figure 1.

Figure 1. Add the multi-select group

2) Enter the information of the new multi-select group, and click <OK>.

3) The new group information will be displayed in the “Multi-select” dialog box. We can initiate a call to the new multi-select group.

Note:

  • The system allows up to three multi-select groups to be created.

  • A multi-select group should contain at least one talk group.

  • If you close the “Multi-select” dialog box, the customized multi-select group information will disappear and won’t be saved.

The steps of modifying the multi-select group are as follows:

1) Select the multi-select group to be modified in the “Multi-select” dialog box.

2) Click <Modify>. The “Modify Members” dialog box pops up, shown in Figure 2.

Figure 2. Modify the multi-select group

3) Modifying the information based on actual needs, and click <OK>.

4) The modified group information will be displayed in the “Multi-select” dialog box.

The steps of multi-select are as follows:

1) Select the group to be multi-selected in the “Multi-select” dialog box.

2) Click <Multi-select>. An icon will appear in the right of the multi-select group, shown in Figure 3.

Figure 3. Multi-select

The steps of initiating a multi-select group call are as follows:

1) Press <PTT> in the “Multi-select” dialog box to initiate the call, shown in Figure 4. All the multi-selected group members will receive the voices.

Figure 4. Initiate the multi-select group call

2) Release <PTT> to end the call, shown in Figure 5.

Figure 5. End the multi-select group call

The steps of cancelling the multi-select are as follows:

1) Select the multi-select group to be cancelled in the “Multi-select” dialog box.

2) Click <Cancel>. The icon in the right of the multi-select group will disappear, shown in Figure 6.

Figure 6. Cancel the multi-select

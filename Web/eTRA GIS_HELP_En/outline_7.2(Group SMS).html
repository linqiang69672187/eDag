Group SMS

  • Send Group SMS

  • Receive Group SMS

The steps of sending group SMS are as follows:

1) Right-click a mobile user on the map and select [Application service/Group SMS]. The “Send SMS” dialog box pops up. Enter the content, and choose whether to add other groups, shown in Figure 1. Then click <Send>.

Figure 1. Send group SMS

2) Alternatively, click [Dispatch/Call panel] on the menu bar and click "SMS". The “Send SMS” dialog box pops up, shown in Figure 2. Click <Select> to select the group, and enter the content. Then click <Send>.

Figure 2. “Send SMS” dialog box

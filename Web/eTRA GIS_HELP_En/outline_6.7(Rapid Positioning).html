Rapid Positioning

Through the rapid positioning function, we can get the target user’s latitude and longitude information.

The steps of rapid positioning are as follows:

1) Click [Dispatch/Call panel] on the menu bar and click "Rapid positioning”. The “Rapid positioning” dialog box pops up, shown as below.

Rapid positioning

2) Enter the terminal ID, or click <Select> to select the user.

3) Click <OK>. The user's radio will report a message of latitude and longitude information to the GPS resolution server.

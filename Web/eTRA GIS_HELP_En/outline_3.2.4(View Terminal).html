View Terminal

The steps of viewing the terminal are as follows:

1) Click [Basis/Terminal]. The Terminal List pops up.

2) Select the unit and click <Search>. The list will display all information of the terminals of this unit, shown in Figure 1.

Figure 1. Query the Terminal List

3) Enter the terminal ID and click <Search>. The list will quickly retrieve and display all information of the terminals containing this ID, shown in Figure 2.

Figure 2. Query the terminals

4) Click the terminal ID to display the details, shown in Figure 3.

Figure 3. View the terminal information

Note:

  • The first-level dispatcher can view the information of all terminals.

  • The hierarchical-level dispatcher can only view the terminal information of his unit and sub-units.

  • If the terminal does not exist, the system will prompt, "No data! "

Modify Dispatcher

The steps of modifying the dispatcher are as follows:

1) Click [Basis/Dispatcher]. The Dispatcher List pops up.

2) Click <Modify> in the right of the dispatcher, and the “Modify” dialog box pops up. Modify the dispatcher information based on actual needs, shown as below.

3) Click <OK>. If succeeded, the system will prompt the success; if failed, it will prompt the cause of error.

Modify the dispatcher information

Note:

  • The first-level dispatcher can modify the dispatcher information at all levels.

  • The hierarchical-level dispatcher can only modify the dispatcher information of his unit and sub-units.

  • Once a dispatcher is created, the dispatcher name cannot be modified.

  • The information of the logged dispatchers cannot be modified.

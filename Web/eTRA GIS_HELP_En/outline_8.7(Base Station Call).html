Base Station Call

The base station call is namely the broadcast call. It is initiated under the coverage of one or more base stations. All terminals within the scope can receive the call, while they cannot transmit voices.

In the system, the base station call includes:

  • Single-Station Call

  • Multi-Station Call

  • All-Station Call

Note:

The killed radios cannot receive the base station call.

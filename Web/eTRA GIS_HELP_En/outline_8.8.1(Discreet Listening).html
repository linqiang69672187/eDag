Discreet Listening

  • Listen to Full-Duplex Call

  • Listen to Half-Duplex Call

The steps of listening to a full-duplex call are as follows:

1) Click [Dispatch/Call panel] on the menu bar and click “Discreet listening”. The “Discreet listening” dialog box pops up.

2) Enter the terminal ID, such as 180008. The system will get the corresponding information automatically, shown in Figure 1.

Figure 1. “Discreet listening” dialog box

3) Click <Start>. Then the user is in the state of being monitored, shown in Figure 2.

Figure 2. Discreet listening is activated

4) When the user is in a full-duplex call, the dispatcher can monitor the call. Besides, the dispatcher can break in or release the call, shown in Figure 3.

Figure 3. Listen to a full-duplex call

5) When the full-duplex call ends, or the dispatcher clicks <End>, the discreet listening will end, shown in Figure 4.

Figure 4. End the discreet listening

The steps of listening to a half-duplex call are as follows:

1) Click [Dispatch/Call panel] on the menu bar and click “Discreet listening”. The “Discreet listening” dialog box pops up.

2) Enter the terminal ID, such as 180008. The system will get the corresponding information automatically.

3) Click <Start> to activate the monitoring. Then the user is in the state of being monitored.

4) When the user is in a half-duplex call, the dispatcher can monitor the call. Besides, the dispatcher can break in or release the call, shown in Figure 5.

Figure 5. Listen to a half-duplex call

5) For example, “180008” and “160001” are in the half-duplex call. “180008” can hold down the PTT button to obtain the transmission permission, and release the PTT button to release the permission, shown in Figure 6. Then, “160001” can hold down the PTT button to obtain the permission, shown in Figure 7.

Figure 6. "180008" releases permission

Figure 7. "160001" obtains permission

6) When the half-duplex call ends, or the dispatcher clicks <End>, the discreet listening will end.

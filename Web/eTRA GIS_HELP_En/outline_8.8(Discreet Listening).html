Discreet Listening

The dispatcher can monitor the communications of a mobile user without being noticed. When the monitored party is in a call, the dispatcher can listen, break in or release the call.

The monitoring ends in the following cases:

  • The monitoring party takes the initiative to end the monitoring.

  • The monitored call is disconnected.

Note:

  • At a time, the dispatch console could only monitor one mobile user.

  • It will fail to monitor an unregistered mobile user.

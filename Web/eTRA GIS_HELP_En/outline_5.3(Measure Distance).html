Measure Distance

The system supports measuring both the distance and area.

The steps of measurement are as follows:

1) Click “” in the map toolbar.

2) The measurement mode selection box "" will pop up. Click “" to measure the distance, or click “" to measure the area.

3) Click on the map to determine the measurement starting point; release the mouse and move, and click other points one by one as the measurement points; double-click to end the measurement, shown in Figure 1 and Figure 2.

3) Click “” to close the distance measurement.

Figure 1. Measure the distance

Figure 2. Measure the area

Note:

  • The system supports continuous measuring.

  • The unit of measurement is m and ㎡.

  • Zooming in and out of the map has no effect on the accuracy of the measurement.

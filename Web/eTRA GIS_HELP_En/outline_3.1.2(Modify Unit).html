Modify Unit

Only the first-level dispatcher can modify the unit information at all levels.

The steps of modifying the unit are as follows:

1) Click [Basis/Unit]. The Unit List pops up.

2) Click <Modify> in the right of the unit, and the “Modify” dialog box pops up. Modify the unit information based on actual needs, shown as below.

3) Click <OK>. If succeeded, the system will prompt the success; if failed, it will prompt the cause of error.

Note:

Parameter "The higher-level unit" and "Subordinate level" cannot be modified.

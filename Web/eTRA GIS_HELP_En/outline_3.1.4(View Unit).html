View Unit

The steps of viewing the unit are as follows:

1) Click [Basis/Unit]. The Unit List pops up.

2) Select the unit and click <Search>. The list will display the sub-units of this unit, shown in Figure 1.

Figure 1. Query the Unit List

3) Alternatively, enter the unit name and click <Search>. The list will quickly retrieve and display the basic information of the unit. Dispatcher can delete, modify, locate the unit or add subordinate units.

4) Click the unit name in the list. It will display the detailed information of this unit, shown In Figure 2.

Figure 2. ViewÂ the unit information

Note:

  • The first-level dispatcher can view the unit information at all levels.

  • The hierarchical-level dispatcher can only view the unit information of his unit and sub-units.

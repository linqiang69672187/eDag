View Patch Group

The steps of viewing the patch group are as follows:

1) Click [Marshaling/Patch group]. The Patch Group List pops up.

2) Select the unit, or enter the patch group name. Click <Search>. The list will display the patch group information, shown in Figure 1.

Figure 1. Query the Patch Group List

3) Click the patch group name to display the details, shown in Figure 2.

View Dispatch Console

The steps of viewing the dispatch console are as follows:

1) Click [Basis/Dispatch console]. The Dispatch Console List pops up.

2) Select the unit, or enter the dispatch console ID. Click <Search>. The list will display the dispatch console information, shown in Figure 1.

Figure 1. Query the Dispatch Console List

3) Click the dispatch console ID to display the details, shown in Fifure 2.

Single-Station Call

Single-station call is to initiate a call to a single base station.

The steps of initiating a single-station call are as follows:

1) Click [Dispatch/BS call] on the menu bar and click “BS call". The “BS call” dialog box pops up, shown in Figure 1.

Figure 1. “BS call” dialog box

2) Select "Single-station call" and enter the BS ID, and then click <Call>, shown in Figure 2.

Figure 2. Initiate the single-station call

3) Click <End call> to end the call, shown in Figure 3.

Figure 3. End the single-station call

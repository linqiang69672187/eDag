Delete Multicast Group

The steps of deleting the multicast group are as follows:

1) Click [Marshaling/Multicast group]. The Multicast Group List pops up.

2) Click <Delete> in the right of the multicast group. A confirmation window pops up.

3) Click <OK>. If succeeded, the system will prompt the success; if failed, it will prompt the cause of error.

Note:

  • If a multicast group is in a call, then it cannot be deleted.

  • Before deleting a multicast group, check whether the multicast group is contained in a multi-select/dynamic group, whether it is associated with the terminal information.

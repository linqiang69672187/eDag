The steps of customizing the patch group are as follows:

1) Click <Custom> in the “Patch” dialog box. The “Add” dialog box pops up, shown in Figure 1.

Figure 1. Add the patch group

2) Enter the information of the new patch group, and click <OK>.

3) The new group information will be displayed in the “Patch” dialog box. We can initiate a call to the new patch group.

Note:

  • The system allows up to eight patch groups to be created.

  • A patch group should contain at least one talkgroup.

  • If you close the “Patch” dialog box, the customized patch group information will disappear and won’t be saved.

The steps of modifying the patch group are as follows:

1) Select the patch group to be modified in the “Patch” dialog box.

2) Click <Modify>. The “Modify Members” dialog box pops up, shown in Figure 2.

Figure 2. Modify the patch group

3) Modifying the information based on actual needs, and click <OK>.

4) The modified group information will be displayed in the “Patch” dialog box.

The steps of patch are as follows:

1) Select the group to be patched in the “Patch” dialog box.

2) Click <Patch>. An icon will appear in the right of the patch group, and the status of the group members is “Patched”, shown in Figure 3.

Figure 3. Patch

The steps of initiating a patch group call are as follows:

1) Press <PTT> in the “Patch” dialog box to initiate the call, shown in Figure 4. All the patched group members will receive the voices.

Figure 4. Initiate the patch group call

2) Release <PTT> to release the transmission permission, shown in Figure 5.

Figure 5. Dispatcher releases permission

3) Members in the patch group can hold down the PTT button to preempt the permission, shown in Figure 6.

Figure 6. Group member obtains permission

4) Click <End> to end the call, shown in Figure 7.

Figure 7. End the patch group call

The steps of cancelling the patch are as follows:

1) Select the patch group to be cancelled in the “Patch” dialog box.

2) Click <Cancel>. The icon in the right of the patch group will disappear, and the status of the group members is “Unpatched”, shown in Figure 8.

Figure 8. Cancel the patch

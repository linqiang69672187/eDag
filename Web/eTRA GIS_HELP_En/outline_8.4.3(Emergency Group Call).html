Emergency Group Call

  • Call Panel: Initiate Emergency Group Call; Receive Emergency Group Call

  • Uplink Call Panel: Receive Emergency Group Call; Initiate Emergency Group Call

The steps of initiating an emergency group call through call panel are as follows:

1) Click [Dispatch/Call panel] on the menu bar and click "Emergency call”. The “Emergency call” dialog box pops up.

2) Enter the group ID, such as 2490001. The system will get the corresponding information automatically. Click <PTT> to initiate the group call.

3) Press <PTT> to speak, shown in Figure 1.1.

Figure 1.1. Dispatcher obtains permission

4) After the dispatcher releases <PTT>, members in the group user can hold down the PTT button to speak, shown in Figure 1.2.

Figure 1.2. Group member obtains permission

5) Click <End> to end the call.

Note:

It will fail to initiate an emergency group call if there is no online user in the group.

The steps of receiving an emergency group call through call panel are as follows:

1) When the dispatch console receives a group call from the user 180008, who belongs to the group shenyang1 (2490001), the “Emergency call” dialog box is shown in Figure 1.3.

Figure 1.3. Call access request

2) The dispatch console can receive the voices directly. After the group member releases the PTT button, the dispatcher can press <PTT> to speak.

3) Click <End> to end the call.

The steps of receiving an emergency group call through uplink call panel are as follows:

1) When the dispatch console receives an emergency group call from a user, such as user 180008, who belongs to group shenyang1 (2490001), the group call panel is shown in Figure 2.1.

Figure 2.1. Call access request

2) The dispatch console can receive the voices directly. After the group member releases the PTT button, the dispatcher can press <PTT> to speak, shown in Figure 2.2.

Figure 2.2. Dispatcher obtains permission

3) Click <End> to end the call.

The steps of initiating an emergency group call through uplink call panel are as follows:

1) Press <PTT> in the private call panel to initiate the call.

2) After the dispatcher releases <PTT>, members in the group can hold down the PTT button to preempt the transmission permission, shown in Figure 2.3.

Figure 2.3. Mobile user obtains permission

3) Click <End> to end the call.

Note:

In the uplink call panel, you can only initiate an emergency group call to the terminal that already has the emergency group call records.

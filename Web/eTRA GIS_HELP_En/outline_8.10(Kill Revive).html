Kill/Revive

The dispatch console can kill the unauthorized radio in the network, and the killed radio cannot perform any operation. If the radio is powered off when being killed, it will be automatically killed when powered on.

Revive is a reverse action of kill. The system issues the recovery instruction, and the killed radio will recover to the normal working status after receiving the instruction. If the radio is powered off when being revived, it will automatically recover the trunking working mode when powered on.

The steps of killing/reviving a radio are as follows:

1) Click [Dispatch/Call panel] on the menu bar and click "Kill/Revive”. The "Kill/Revive” dialog box pops up.

2) Enter the terminal ID, such as 24004. The system will get the corresponding information automatically, shown in Figure 1.

Figure 1. “Kill/Revive” dialog box

3) Click <Kill>. A prompt box pops up, shown in Figure 2.

Figure 2. Kill

4) If you want to revive the killed radio, click <Revive> in the “Kill/Revive” dialog box. A prompt box pops up, shown in Figure 3.

Figure 3. Revive

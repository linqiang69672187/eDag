Modify Patch Group

The steps of modifying the patch group are as follows:

1) Click [Marshaling/Patch group]. The Patch Group List pops up.

2) Click <Modify> in the right of the patch group, and the “Modify” dialog box pops up, shown as below. Modify the patch group information based on actual needs.

3) Click <OK>. If succeeded, the system will prompt the success; if failed, it will prompt the cause of error.

Modify the patch group information

Note:

  • Once a patch group is created, the "The unit" parameter cannot be modified.

  • If a patch group is in a call, then it cannot be modified.

  • The dispatcher could only modify the patch group information of his unit and sub-units.

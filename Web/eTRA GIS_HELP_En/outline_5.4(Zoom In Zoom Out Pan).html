Zoom In/Zoom Out/Pan

There are two operation methods to zoom the map in:

1) Click “” in the map toolbar. Select a region on the map and this region will be zoomed in two levels.

2) Scroll forward to zoom the map in.

There are two operation methods to zoom the map out:

1) Click “” in the map toolbar. Select a region on the map and this region will be zoomed out two levels.

2) Scroll backward to zoom the map out.

The steps of panning the map are as follows:

1) Determine a central point on the map.

2) Hold down the left mouse button to pan the map as needed.

3) Release the mouse.

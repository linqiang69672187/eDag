The steps of selecting an individual user are as follows:

1) Click a user on the map. An icon will appear in the upper right corner of the user, shown in Figure 1.

Figure 1. The selected user

2) Click another user to select a new one and deselect the original one.

3) Click anywhere on the map to deselect the user. The icon will disappear.

The steps of selecting multiple users are as follows:

1) Hold down the Ctrl key, and click the users one by one. The icons will appear in the upper right corner of the selected users.

2) Click anywhere on the map to deselect the users. The icons will disappear.

The steps of selecting all users in a region are as follows:

1) Click “” in the map toolbar.

2) Select a region on the map, and all users (including the displayed and the hidden) in this region will be listed, shown in Figure 2.

3) You can display/hide/locate the users, and perform dispatch/SMS/application services. You can also click the name of the user to view the user details.

Figure 2. Region selection

Note:

  • Region selection is particularly applicable when a large number of police officers are overlapped and indistinguishable.

  • Region selection supports bulk SMS. When an emergency occurs in a region, dispatcher can quickly select the police officers near the region, and send mass messages to them to issue the instruction.

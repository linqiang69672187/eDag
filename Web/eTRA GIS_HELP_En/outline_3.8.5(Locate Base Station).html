Locate Base Station

The steps of locating the base station are as follows:

1) Click [Basis/BS]. The Base Station List pops up.

2) In the “Locate” column, click the icon, the map’s center point will immediately locate to the base station's current position on the map, and mark with a red flag, shown as below.

Locate the base station

Note:

If the latitude and longitude of a base station is 0 or is not configured, then it cannot be located.

Add Terminal

The steps of adding the terminal are as follows:

1) Click [Basis/Terminal]. The Terminal List pops up, shown in Figure 1.

Figure 1. Terminal List

2) Click <Add>, and the “Add” dialog box pops up. Enter the terminal information in the dialog box, shown in Figure 2.

3) Click <OK>. If succeeded, the system will prompt the success; if failed, it will prompt the cause of error.

Figure 2. Add the terminal information

The steps of adding the terminals in batches are as follows:

1) Click [Basis/Terminal]. The Terminal List pops up.

2) Click <Bulk Add>, and the “Bulk Add” dialog box pops up. Click <Add> in the dialog box, and enter the ID range of the terminals. Add the scan group and multicast group based on actual needs, shown in

Figure 3.

3) Click <OK>.

Figure 3. Add the terminals in batches

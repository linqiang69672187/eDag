Multicast Group Call

The multicast group is composed of a group of talkgroups. The operations of multicast group call and group call are similar.

The steps of initiating a multicast group call are as follows:

1) Click [Dispatch/Call panel] on the menu bar, and select “Group call”. The “Group call” dialog box pops up.

2) Enter the multicast group ID or click <Select> in the right to select the multicast group.

3) Press <PTT> to transmit voices. All group members in the multicast group can receive the voices.

4) After the dispatcher releases <PTT>, members in the multicast group can hold down the PTT button to preempt the permission.

5) Click <End> to end the call.

Note:

It will fail to initiate a multicast group call if there is no online user in the multicast group.
